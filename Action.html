Personal Lifestyle Changes


  • Increase Cyber Awareness: Educate yourself about common cyber threats such as phishing, malware, and data breaches. Understanding how machine learning (ML) algorithms detect anomalies can help you recognize suspicious activities.

  • Adopt Secure Online Practices: Use strong, unique passwords and enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) to secure your accounts. ML algorithms often detect unauthorized access by identifying unusual login behaviors.

  • Update Regularly: Keep your devices, software, and applications updated to reduce vulnerabilities. Machine learning algorithms can better protect you if your systems are up-to-date.
