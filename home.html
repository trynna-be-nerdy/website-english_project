Importance of Machine Learning in Data Security

Machine Learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being "explicitly programmed (Mr.Clark 2025)." In the context of data security, Machine Learning algorithms can analyze vast amounts of data, identify patterns, and detect "anomalies(Mr.Clark 2025)" or an inconsistency with the data, which may indicate unauthorized access or potential breaches. By continuously learning from new data, these algorithms can adapt to evolving threats and enhance the overall security posture of organizations.



Impacts of Data breaches to the people

According to a research study conducted by Lorenzaj, as data breaches become more common, leveraging advanced technologies like Machine Learning is essential to ensure the security and integrity of sensitive information. In 2023, the number of data compromises in the United States stood at 3,205 cases. Meanwhile, over 353 million individuals were affected in the same year by data compromises, including data breaches, leakage, and exposure(Lorenzaj 2024). While these are three different events, they have one thing in common. As a result of all three incidents, the sensitive data is accessed by an unauthorized individual.
